# buzzcut

**Buzzcut** marks a reset — a fresh start and the beginning of my Software Engineer bootcamp journey.

## About

- **Origin**: repurposed from my instructor‑led bootcamp repo  
- **Dual Purpose**:  
  - Clean, modular dev environment for bootcamp projects and assignments  
    - Course content in `/bootcamp` is excluded via `.gitignore` for Codecademy copyright  
  - **Clonable**: designed for new coders seeking a baseline repo  
    - Uses HTMLHint instead of W3C Validator, which conflicted with Prettier
- **Philosophy**: clarity, structure, and reproducibility — balanced with an MVP mindset and a focus on bigger‑picture impact

## Projects

This repo will grow to include:
- Responsive layouts and semantic HTML builds  
- Full‑stack projects from bootcamp and beyond  
- Configurations and workflows that support reproducibility and maintainability

## Why “Buzzcut”

As a veteran, a buzzcut is often the first step in bootcamp — a symbolic reset before the real journey begins.  
For me, Buzzcut represents stripping things down to essentials and building forward with purpose.
